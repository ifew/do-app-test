SIVILAI

[civilize, sivilaiz, ศิวิไล, 使文明] / sɪv.ɪ.laɪz / verb.

Definition

  • to raise from barbarism to an enlightened stage of development; bring out of a primitive or savage state.
  • to educate a society so that its culture becomes more developed.
  • to improve someone's behaviour.

SIVILAI group is a group of friends and we want to see Thai people (and/or international people) improve life of themselves.
